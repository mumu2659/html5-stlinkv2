Cannot Access ST-Link
WebUSB cannot obtain device access. On macOS, libusb (st-info / st-flash tools) and Chrome WebUSB compete for USB Interface, and the kernel may still hold the handle even after the process exits.

Most reliable solution: Clear processes first, then physically unplug ST-Link USB.
Step 1 · Execute in Terminal
pkill -f "ST-LINK_gdbserver"; pkill -f "STLinkUSBDriver"; pkill -f "st-util" Click to copy
Step 2 · Unplug USB
🔌
Unplug ST-Link USB cable from Mac
Wait 2 seconds, then replug
After you hear the system connection sound
Step 3 · Reconnect
After unplugging, immediately click "Reconnect" button below.
Note: Do not run st-info / st-flash commands after unplugging.
